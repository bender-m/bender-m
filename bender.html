About Me

My name is Bender Mustefa, and I am a Grade 11 student at Ethiopian International School Riyadh. I believe that school is one of the most important parts of life because it helps students gain knowledge, improve their personalities, and prepare for the future.

Being a student is not only about studying subjects like mathematics, science, and English, but also about learning important life skills such as teamwork, leadership, communication, and responsibility. I always try to balance my education with hobbies and extracurricular activities because they make life more enjoyable and meaningful.

Students should participate in activities outside the classroom because they teach lessons that cannot always be learned from books alone.

My Hobbies

Outside of school, I have several hobbies that are an important part of my life. Here are the activities I enjoy most and what each one has taught me:

  • ⚽
    Football My favorite sport. It teaches teamwork, discipline, patience, and how to stay calm under pressure. It also helps me stay physically healthy and relieve stress after school.
  • 🎵
    Listening to Music Music helps me relax and stay motivated. I sometimes listen while studying because it helps me concentrate and remain calm.
  • 🍳
    Cooking A useful and creative skill. Preparing meals for family and friends teaches patience, organization, and responsibility, and gives me a sense of independence.
